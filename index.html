Who We Are

We love traveling, 4 years ago we decided to visit Africa for its beautiful views, nature and wild life. We decided to explore the more underdeveloped areas away from common tourist spots to see Africa in its true nature.

What We Do

Here, we discovered an old, small school containing 30-50 students. Despite being in a poor condition institution, the students were very driven to learn and to work-hard. Inspired by this, we decided to build a better, well-conditioned school outside of town to easily reach the children, along with conducting medical support to further help a wide variety of people. Now, we have hundreds of students partaking in our school and established stronger connections from many people within Uganda.
